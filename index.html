INFORMATION:

- Do you only make scripts for QBCore/QBox?

Yes, currently I only make scripts for QBCore and QBox due to only experimenting with those frameworks. However I do want to expand into other frameworks for those who don't want use the QBCore or QBox framework. This will be something I'll get around to at some point but at the moment I'm continuing with QBCore and QBox till I'm confident enough to tackle another framework.

- Are all your scripts free?

Yes, currently all scripts I'm making are completely free and open-source. I'm in my experiementing phase so everything I make is just me learning new things. I do plan to make scripts to sell but that's not for a while now. When I'm confident enough to make bigger and better scripts that's when I'll start. For now script development is just a hobby I do in my spare time.
